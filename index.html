🐉 What is DBA?

Dragon Ball Asylum is a competitive multiplayer RPG where you create a fighter, pick a race, and grind your way from Level 1 all the way to Champion rank. The game revolves around a wipe cycle - each wipe lasts roughly 3 months. During that time, everyone starts fresh and races to the top. At the end of each wipe, a special tournament is held with real cash prizes, in-game rewards for the next wipe, and a permanent statue in the Hall of Fame.

Along the way, you'll be training stats, unlocking transformations, completing quest medals, joining guilds, farming mobs, entering events, and competing in PvP. There's a lot to take in — that's what this wiki is for. Use the sidebar tabs to explore each system in detail.

🌍 What You'll Encounter

DBA has a lot of systems working together. Here's the short version of what you'll run into as you play:

Training & Stats — Core stats like Powerlevel, Ki, Strength, Defense, and Stamina. Raise them through Self Train, Shadow Spar, Meditation, and spending Training Points. Higher gravity rooms give better gains.

Rebirths — The progression system. You'll rebirth multiple times (0/3 through 6/3), resetting your level but keeping currencies and progress. The Walkthrough tab walks you through every step.

Races & Transformations — Choose a race at character creation. Each has its own transformation tree. Later you'll unlock Ultra forms and Dual Genes.

Currencies — Zenni, ZP, Training Points, EK, Ultra Points, Casino Tokens, and more. ZP is critical for level caps and gear.

Events & PvP — Sagas, Mayhem, tournaments, CTF, Mystical Cavern, Conquest, RBA, and more.

Quest Medals & Ranks — 53 Quest Medals earned from NPCs. You need them to break past level caps. Rank (Easy → Champion) is tied to level and medal count.

Guilds — Team up with other players. Guilds have houses, upgradeable facilities, evolution paths, and guild-specific events.

Skill Bar & Skillcards

Techniques use the Skill Bar and Skillcard GUI. Assign: Click an empty slot → select technique · Use: Press 1–9 · Target: Spacebar · Untarget: U

Keys 1–9, Spacebar, and U are hard-coded. Delete any macros bound to these keys to avoid conflicts.

  • NPC talk: Press Spacebar while directly next to them.

📊 Stats & Abilities
Core Stats
  • Powerlevel (PL): Your health — reaches 0 = KO/killed. Also controls the max amount of Ki, Ki Defense, Strength, and Defense you can have.
  • Ki: Strength of ki attacks
  • Ki Defense: Defence vs. ki attacks
  • Strength: Strength of melee attacks
  • Defense: Defence vs. melee attacks
  • Fatigue (Stamina): How long you can train. Restore with treadmills
Abilities (talk to Hercule | cap: 75%)
  • Block: Chance to block melee attacks
  • Counter: Chance to counter melee attacks
  • Critical: Chance to score a critical hit
  • Dodge: Chance to dodge ki attacks
  • Reflect: Chance to reflect ki attacks
Other Stats
  • Level: Your current level. Raise through training, mobbing, and events
  • TNL (Till Next Level): EXP required to reach your next level. TNL Cuts (ZP) reduce this number
  • True Level (TL): Total levels this rebirth. Used for Dual Gene (10.5m TL) and Ultra Points (1m TL = 1 UP)
  • Age: 1 hour real time = 1 month, 12 months = 1 year
  • Universe: Which of the 4 Universes you belong to. G.o.D title holder leads the Universe
  • Karma: Good or Evil. Required for NPC boosts (Guru = 25% Good, Babidi = 100% Evil)

Sagas

Sagas spawn mobs across the world in timeline order through the anime story arcs. There are two sides: Evil and Good. Sagas auto-run after one completes.

  • Difficulty Levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, Extreme, Insane, Godly. Higher difficulty = stronger mob stats, better EXP, and smarter AI
  • Change difficulty at Yamcha or the Mall Mascot
  • Some saga mobs are stronger than normal and have quests attached — keep an eye out and circle back to complete them!
Mayhem

Open PvP free-for-all event. Earn Guild Points by participating. One of the main sources of GP alongside Guild Tournament.

Ranked Battle Arena (RBA)

Competitive 1v1 tournament bracket. Win matches to earn RBA Tokens, which exchange for ZP (10 ZP per exchange) at the PVP Broker in the Mall.

Tournament of Power (ToP)

Universe vs Universe elimination event. Earn Universe Points (UP) for participation and wins.

Winner: 1,500 levels + 50 ZP + 25 Universe Points

Mystical Cavern

Dungeon event. Earn Cavern Stat Pills (6× stat gain for 30 min) and other rewards. Crocodile Meat (from Roshi's island) is useful for healing inside. Golden Frieza spawns here with a 50% chance to drop the Cavern Stat Pill.

Conquest

Territory control event. Win zones to earn Universe Points. Guild coordination is important for Conquest.

Kill the King

Your stats are balanced and set for this event. Kill the opposing team's King while protecting yours at the same time. Play strategically!

Loser: Levels + Zenni + 1 PVP Token  ·  Winner: Levels + Zenni + 3 PVP Tokens

Team Deathmatch (TDM)

Team PvP event. Coordinate with your team to eliminate the opposing side.

Pillar Event

Universe members sign up to protect their pillar and destroy other universe towers. Your stats are balanced and set for this event. Unlimited respawns until your tower is destroyed.

Lower reward: +1,500 levels · +50 ZP · +100 Universe Points

Grand Maze

Players sign up for a loot bag hunt, followed by forced PvP once 4 players remain. Loot bags can contain a wide variety of rewards. Player kills drop better loot bags.

Red Ribbon Battle Tower

Your stats are balanced and set for this event. Collect both the Red Star and Blue Star Dragon Balls, then enter the Red Ribbon Tower. Killing players will drop them. Exit the Forest of Chaos to win.

Loser reward: +750 levels + 1 PVP Token

Daily Quests

Available every 18 hours. Complete quests for Daily Quest Tokens (DQ). Spend at the DQ Rewards Store for:

  • Cavern Stat Pill (12 tokens)
  • Club Ticket (10 tokens)
  • Reset Hakai Cooldown (10 tokens)
  • Stat boosts, TNL modifiers, pills
Capture the Flag (CTF)

Team-based flag capture event. Coordinate with your team to grab the enemy flag and return it to base.

Bounty Hunter

Earn rewards by reaching kill milestones. At 300 kills you earn a Club Ticket. Rewards scale with kill count — check the Bounty Hunter NPC for current thresholds.

Guilds
💡 Remember: Leave your guild (or transfer ownership) before every rebirth. Failing to do so may cause issues with your rebirth.
🏰 Guild Basics

Guilds are player-run groups. They have their own Guild House, upgradeable facilities, a shared Guild Bank, and compete in Guild Events for Guild Points (GP).

  • Create a Guild: Use the Create Guild verb. Costs Zenni.
  • Join a Guild: Get invited by a Guild Leader or Co-Leader
  • Guild Rank Roles: Leader → Co-Leader → Member
  • Guild Bank: Shared Zenni pool. Officers and Leaders can deposit/withdraw.
  • Guild House: Private area for guild members with training facilities.
🏅 Guild Points (GP)

GP is the guild progression currency. Sources:

  • Guild Tournament — Primary GP source
  • Mayhem — GP Flag offers some of the best GP income
  • Agent X — 100 UP → 25 GP

Spend GP on Guild Upgrades and Guild Evolution paths.

⚡ Guild Evolutions

Guilds can evolve along different paths, each providing unique bonuses to all members:

  • Hustlers — Mob Farming w/ ZP Suits +50% | TP Gains when Spending +25% | Level Up Stat Gains +10% | Oracle Fish EXP chance 20%→50% | Stat Storage +10% per 100b
  • Mobsters — Mob Farming EXP +30% | Player Kill EXP +50% | Stat Pills: Red/Blue x2.5 (was x2); Cavern x3.5 (was x3) | +50% Stat Pill Duration | +10% Bonus if Active Mob Kill Boost awarded
  • Gym Bunny — Candy farming +50% | Stat Training w/ Equipment: +30% active / +20% AFK | Club House duration +100% (60 min total) | 50% Zenni Discount on Stat Candies | 20% Zenni Discount on Legendary Gym Equipment

Guild evolution paths may be reset and changed for 5k GP.
